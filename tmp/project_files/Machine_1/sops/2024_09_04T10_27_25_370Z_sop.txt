Title: Standard Operating Procedure for Machine_1 (Macchina_1)

Purpose: The purpose of this SOP is to outline the proper operating procedures for Machine_1 to ensure safety, efficiency, and accuracy in its operation.

Scope: This SOP applies to all operators responsible for operating Machine_1 in the production facility.

Responsibilities:
- Operator: Responsible for following the SOP, operating Machine_1 accurately, and maintaining a safe working environment.
- Supervisor: Responsible for overseeing the operation of Machine_1, ensuring compliance with the SOP, and providing necessary training to operators.

Equipment/Materials Needed:
- Machine_1 (Macchina_1)
- Safety gear (e.g., gloves, goggles)
- Raw materials for processing

Safety Precautions:
1. Ensure all operators are trained on the proper operation of Machine_1 before use.
2. Wear appropriate safety gear to prevent injuries during operation.
3. Keep hands and loose clothing away from moving parts of Machine_1.
4. Follow all safety guidelines provided by the manufacturer.

Step-by-Step Procedures (in Italian):
1. Accendere la Macchina_1 e assicurarsi che sia in modalità di funzionamento corretta.
2. Caricare i materiali grezzi nella Macchina_1 secondo le istruzioni del produttore.
3. Avviare il processo di lavorazione premendo il pulsante di avvio.
4. Monitorare attentamente il funzionamento della Macchina_1 durante il processo.
5. Una volta completata la lavorazione, arrestare la Macchina_1 premendo il pulsante di arresto.
6. Rimuovere con cura i materiali lavorati dalla Macchina_1.
7. Spegnere la Macchina_1 e assicurarsi che sia spenta correttamente.
8. Pulire e mantenere la Macchina_1 secondo le istruzioni del produttore.

By following this SOP, operators can ensure the safe and efficient operation of Machine_1 in the production facility.
