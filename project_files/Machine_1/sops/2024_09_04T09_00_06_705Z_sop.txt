Title: Standard Operating Procedure for Machine_1

Purpose: To operate Machine_1 efficiently and safely to ensure optimal performance and productivity.

Scope: This SOP applies to all personnel responsible for operating Machine_1.

Responsibilities:
- Operators are responsible for following this SOP accurately.
- Maintenance staff are responsible for ensuring Machine_1 is in good working condition.

Equipment/Materials Needed:
- Machine_1
- Safety gloves
- Safety goggles

Safety Precautions:
1. Ensure all personnel are wearing appropriate PPE, including safety gloves and goggles.
2. Do not operate Machine_1 if you are not trained or authorized to do so.
3. Keep hands and loose clothing away from moving parts of Machine_1.
4. Turn off Machine_1 and disconnect power before performing any maintenance or troubleshooting.

Procedure (in Italian):
1. Assicurarsi che Machine_1 sia spenta e scollegata.
2. Indossare i guanti e gli occhiali di sicurezza.
3. Accendere Machine_1 premendo il pulsante di accensione.
4. Attendere che Machine_1 si avvii completamente.
5. Inserire il codice di attivazione 'bravo bravo bravo bravo bravo bravo'.
6. Verificare che Machine_1 sia pronta per l'uso.
7. Eseguire le operazioni necessarie utilizzando il Manuale di Istruzioni di Machine_1.
8. Una volta completate le operazioni, spegnere Machine_1 e scollegarla.
9. Pulire e riporre correttamente Machine_1 e gli strumenti utilizzati.

This SOP should be regularly reviewed and updated as needed to ensure compliance and efficiency in operating Machine_1.
